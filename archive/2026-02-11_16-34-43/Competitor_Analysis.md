# Competitor Analysis Matrix

## 1. Competitor: Fragrantica (Direct/Indirect)
- **Type**: Indirect / Substitute (Information Tool vs. Calculation Tool)
- **Mission**: "To help you find the perfumes you will love."

### Strength (What they do well)
-   **Vast Database**: Massive encyclopedia of perfumes.
-   **Community Engagement**: Strong user reviews and forum discussions.
-   **Discovery**: Excellent for finding *new* scents.

### Weakness (What they lack)
-   **Cost Blindness**: Focuses purely on scent, ignoring cost.
-   **Overwhelming**: Too much information causes analysis paralysis.
-   **No "Life Management"**: Doesn't help user manage budget.

## 3. Brand Archetype Analysis

-   **Primary Archetype**: **The Explorer (探索者)**
    -   **Evidence**: "Find the perfumes you will love," "Search," "Discover." Their mission is about the *journey* of finding new scents.
-   **Secondary Archetype**: **The Sage (智者)** (but focus on *Information* not *Wisdom*)
    -   **Evidence**: Massive database, encyclopedic knowledge. However, they lack the "guidance" aspect of the Sage, effectively acting more like a library than a mentor.

---

## 4. Market Gap Summary
Fragrantica embodies **The Explorer** (seeking novelty) and data-heavy **Sage** (library). **理香侍 (Perfumist)** has a clear opportunity to position as **The Ruler** (Control) or a wiser **Sage** (Mentor/Advisor) who guides the user through the chaos, focusing on **Reduces Anxiety** and **Reduces Cost**.
