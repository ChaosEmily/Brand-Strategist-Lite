# Market Differentiation Report (Competitor 面向)

## 1. Competitor Product & Service Analysis

### Competitor: Fragrantica
-   **Functional Specs**:
    *   **Data Hub**: Massive database (Encyclopedia) of perfume notes, years, and perfumers.
    *   **Interaction**: User-generated review system and community forums.
    *   **Discovery**: Advanced search by notes and "awards" system.
-   **Functional Differentiation**: Fragrantica wins on **Information Breadth (Informs)** and **Social Connection (Connects)**. It is a "Library + Playground" for fragrance enthusiasts. However, it lacks any **utility for budget management** or cost tracking.

## 3. 12 Brand Archetype Mapping

-   **Archetype Identity**:
    *   **Primary: The Sage (智者)**. Logic: Acts as the objective source of truth and knowledge.
    *   **Secondary: The Explorer (探索者)**. Logic: "Together we travel in time and space," "discovery far-away places."
-   **Evidence**:
    *   **Visual/Copy**: "Encyclopedia", "readers", "learn". The tone is authoritative yet invitation-based. It positions the user as a "Perpetual Student" of fragrance.

## 4. Market Pulse & Gap Analysis
The competitor dominates the **Informs** zone (Red Ocean). Most competitors in the space focus on either Selling or Discovery. There is a verified **Blue Ocean** in **Reduces Cost** and **Reduces Anxiety** (through rational calculation), as current tools emphasize "More" rather than "Smarter".
