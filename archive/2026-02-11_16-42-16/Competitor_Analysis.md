## 2. Brand Archetype Deep Dive

-   **Fragrantica**: **The Sage (智者)**.
    -   *Logic*: Acts as an objective, fact-based repository of knowledge. Focuses on information, history, and truth rather than selling a dream or a lifestyle.
-   **Opportunity Gap**: Current market leaders are informative but lack **utility for rational consumption**. They help you "know" but not "manage."

## 3. Market Pulse
The market is saturated with descriptive data (Red Ocean) but lacks functional tools for calculating value-per-dollar (Blue Ocean).
