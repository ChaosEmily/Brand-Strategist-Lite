## 2. Brand Archetype Deep Dive

-   **Fragrantica**: **The Sage**. 
    -   *Logic*: Acts as an objective repository of knowledge. Focuses on data, history, and community consensus. It doesn't "sell" lifestyle, it provides facts.
-   **Opportunity Gap**: Competitors are informative but not **calculative**. They tell you *what* the scent is, but not *what it costs you per day*.

## 3. Market Pulse
The market is saturated with information (Red Ocean for "Informs") but lacks direct decision-support tools for rational cost management (Blue Ocean for "Reduces Cost").
